01 THE AMG STORY

Under the hood, the GT 63 PRO is packing a thunderous AMG 4.0L V8 biturbo engine that's ready to roar. This German powerhouse churns out a whopping 603 hp - that's 26 hp more than its predecessor, the AMG GT 63. What about torque? Well, it's got plenty of that as well - an impressive 627 lb-ft to be precise - ensuring you get that thrilling acceleration every time you hit the gas. This AMG beauty can rocket from 0 to 60 mph in an estimated 3.1 seconds, but what really shows that this thing can pull is that it can carry on to 124 mph in just 10.9 seconds, and then top out at a max speed of 197 mph. That's some serious performance!

The engineers at Affalterbach have also boosted the cooling systems, adding two radiators in the front wheel arches to keep everything running smoothly even during the most intense drives. The cooling for the front and rear differentials, along with the transfer case, is top-notch, thanks to the new geometry of the air vanes and revised brake cover plates. This means you can push the GT 63 PRO to its limits without worrying about overheating - and great for intense track days when you want to summon your inner Lewis Hamilton.
